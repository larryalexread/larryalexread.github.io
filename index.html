About

My research is in analysis, with a focus on spectral and scattering theory. I am interested in semiclassical asymptotics and in applying spectral and scattering methods to settings such as functional inequalities and integrable PDE. I am currently part of the ERC-funded project GEOEDP at Orsay, led by Nicolas Burq.
